Key findings
💰

Income drives purchases

Buyers earn ~5% more than non-buyers. Male buyers average $60,124 vs $56,208 for non-buyers.

🚦

Short commutes win

0–1 mile commuters are the top buyers (200 purchases). Intent drops sharply beyond 5 miles.

🎂

Middle aged sweet spot

Ages 31–54 account for 383 of 481 purchases — only group where buyers outnumber non-buyers.

🌏

Pacific converts best

Despite only 192 customers, Pacific has a 58.9% conversion rate — highest of all three regions.

💼

Professionals lead

150 of 276 professionals bought a bike — 54.3% conversion rate, highest of any occupation.
